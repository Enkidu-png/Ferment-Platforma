## Instructions for Editing

1. **Review each category** - Do the subcategories make sense for your marketplace?
2. **Edit the lists** - Add, remove, or rename subcategories as needed
3. **Keep it consistent** - Try to maintain similar number of subcategories per category (3-6 recommended)
4. **Think about your sellers** - What will they actually be selling?
5. **Consider discoverability** - Will buyers easily find what they're looking for?
